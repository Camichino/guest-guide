Getting Around
🚈
~45–55 min to Midtown
Hudson-Bergen Light Rail → PATH Train
Walk ~5 min to 22nd Street Light Rail station → ride to Exchange Place, Jersey City (~20 min) → transfer to PATH train → Manhattan (~20 min). Fare ~$5.50 total.

NJ Transit Light Rail Info  ·  PATH Schedules & Maps
🚌
~65–80 min to Port Authority / Times Square
Route 119 Bus — Direct to Manhattan
One-seat ride from JFK Blvd stops in Bayonne directly to Port Authority Bus Terminal at 42nd St & 8th Ave — steps from Times Square. Runs every 15–30 min.

NJ Transit Website  ·  Route 119 Real-Time Tracker
⛵
~35–40 min · Scenic Hudson River crossing
NY Waterway Ferry → Brookfield Place
Take Light Rail to Exchange Place (~20 min) → walk 1 block to Paulus Hook Ferry Terminal → ferry to Brookfield Place/Battery Park City (~7 min). Steps off directly at Brookfield Place waterfront entrance. Runs year-round, 7 days a week. Fare ~$10.50 total from property.
